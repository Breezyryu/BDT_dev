## DLC (Double Layer Coating) 상세

DLC technology: **Double Layer Coating**. HED graphite is on the bottom layer and fast charge graphite is on the top layer, which can achieve high kinetic performance.

- 하단층: HED (High Energy Density) graphite
- 상단층: Fast charge graphite
- 효과: 고에너지밀도 + 고율충전 kinetic 성능 동시 확보
